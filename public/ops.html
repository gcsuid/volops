Core Problem

Broken Attendance Ops

Organizations struggle with delayed attendance, unverifiable on-site presence, and poor reporting hygiene across sites/events.

Moat 1: Verified Ground Truth

Each session combines QR token, check-in photo, location check, timer, and checkout log. This creates hard-to-fake volunteer proof.

Moat 2: Site-Level Intelligence

Geofence + activity/site reporting gives operational clarity by location, not just raw attendance counts.

Moat 3: Instant Compliance Exports

Name, Place, Time In, Time Out, Hours Devoted exported to CSV/Excel immediately for grants, audits, and donor reporting.

What We Add

Beyond Logging

VolOps turns volunteer attendance into an operational system: live status visibility, accountability trails, and standardized reporting workflows.
